A Clinic Built on Trust & Compassion

Founded in 2012, Serenova Medical Centre has been at the heart of Nairobi's healthcare landscape. We believe great healthcare is a right — delivered with warmth, precision, and dignity.

Our multidisciplinary team works together to ensure every patient receives personalised, evidence-based care across all stages of life.
